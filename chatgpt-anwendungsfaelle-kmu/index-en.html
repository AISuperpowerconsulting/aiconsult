25 ChatGPT Use Cases for SMEs with Concrete Prompts

ChatGPT can boost productivity in every business area – if you know how. In this guide, you'll find 25 proven use cases for Swiss SMEs, complete with prompt examples for immediate implementation.

Marketing & Content Creation

1. Write Blog Articles

Use Case: Create SEO-optimized blog articles for your website.

Prompt:
"Write an 800-word blog article about [topic] for Swiss SMEs. Target audience: [your audience]. Tone: professional but accessible. Include introduction, 3 main points, and conclusion. Use keyword '[keyword]' 5x naturally."

Time saved: 2-3 hours per article

2. Generate Social Media Posts

Use Case: Create a week's content for LinkedIn, Instagram & Facebook.

Prompt:
"Create 7 social media posts for [platform] about [topic]. Each post max 150 words, with emojis, call-to-action, and 3-5 relevant hashtags. Vary style between informative, entertaining, and inspirational."

Time saved: 3-4 hours per week

3. Product Descriptions

Use Case: Transform technical specs into sales-strong copy.

Prompt:
"Write a compelling product description (200 words) for [product]. Technical details: [details]. Target audience: [audience]. Focus on benefits not features. Add strong call-to-action."

Time saved: 20-30 min per product

Sales & Customer Acquisition

4. Cold Outreach Emails

Use Case: Personalized first-contact emails for potential clients.

Prompt:
"Write a professional outreach email (150 words) for [target company/industry]. Our service: [service]. USP: [3 main benefits]. Tone: respectful but confident. Add clear CTA for initial meeting."

Time saved: 10-15 min per email

5. Proposal Writing

Use Case: Professional proposals for client inquiries.

Prompt:
"Create a professional proposal for [service/product]. Client: [client name/industry]. Scope: [details]. Price: CHF [amount]. Structure: introduction, service description, benefits, price, next steps."

Time saved: 30-45 min per proposal

Customer Support & Communication

6. FAQ Generation

Use Case: Create comprehensive FAQ section.

Prompt:
"Create 15 frequently asked questions and answers about [product/service]. Target audience: [audience]. Answers should be clear, concise, max 100 words. Tone: helpful and friendly."

Time saved: 1-2 hours

7. Complaint Responses

Use Case: Diplomatic responses to customer complaints.

Prompt:
"Write a professional response to this customer complaint: [complaint]. Tone: empathetic, solution-oriented. Structure: apology, show understanding, offer solution, positive closing."

Time saved: 15-20 min per case

Time Savings Calculator

If you regularly use just 5 of these use cases:

  • Blog articles (1x/week): 2.5 hrs
  • Social media (daily): 0.5 hrs/day = 2.5 hrs/week
  • Emails (10x/week): 2 hrs
  • Meeting minutes (3x/week): 1 hr
  • Proposals (2x/week): 1.5 hrs

Total: ~10 hours/week = 40 hours/month

At an hourly rate of CHF 80, that's a value of CHF 3,200/month – for a CHF 20 ChatGPT Plus subscription.
