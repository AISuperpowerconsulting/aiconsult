AI Automation for Trades: More Time for What Matters

Administrative work eats up time you could spend productively on-site. AI automation helps trade businesses focus on their core business. In this guide, we show concrete applications for electricians, carpenters, plumbers, and more.

Why AI Makes Sense Especially for Trade Businesses

Tradespeople spend an average of 30-40% of their working time on administration:

  • Writing quotes (2-3 hrs/week)
  • Phone calls & emails (5-8 hrs/week)
  • Job scheduling & coordination (3-4 hrs/week)
  • Invoicing & reminders (2-3 hrs/week)
  • Material procurement (2-3 hrs/week)

AI can automate 60-80% of this admin work – that's 10-15 hours per week you can use again for customer orders.

The 8 Most Important AI Applications for Trades

1. Automatic Quote Generation

Problem: Writing quotes costs 30-60 min per request

AI Solution: ChatGPT or specialized tools generate quotes from keywords

Prompt Example:
"Create a professional quote for electrical installation in [object]. Services: [list]. Material costs: CHF [amount]. Working hours: [X]. Hourly rate: CHF 95. Structure: materials, labor, total. Payment terms: 30 days net."

Time saved: 20-40 min per quote

2. Intelligent Job Scheduling

Problem: Route planning and appointment coordination are time-consuming

AI Solution: Tools like Craftboxx, PlanRadar use AI for optimal routes

Benefits:

  • 30% less driving time through optimized routes
  • Automatic consideration of traffic & weather
  • Real-time updates for delays

Time saved: 2-3 hrs/week

3. AI Phone Assistant

Problem: Constant calls interrupt work on site

AI Solution: Voice assistants (e.g., Air.ai, Voiceflow) take calls

Functions:

  • Schedule appointments
  • Answer standard questions (hours, prices)
  • Forward urgent cases
  • Take messages

Time saved: 4-6 hrs/week

4. Automatic Invoicing

Problem: Writing invoices and following up takes time

AI Solution: Bexio, Klara with AI extensions

Automation:

  • Generate invoices from order confirmations
  • Automatic payment reminders
  • Fully automated dunning process

Time saved: 2-3 hrs/week

5. Image-Based Damage Analysis

Problem: Preliminary inspections for quotes are time-consuming

AI Solution: Customers send photos, AI analyzes damage

Application:

  • AI recognizes type and extent of damage
  • Estimates required materials
  • Calculates labor hours
  • Generates cost estimate

Time saved: 1-2 hrs per inspection

Initial Situation
