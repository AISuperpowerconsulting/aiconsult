AI Growth 2026: How Swiss SMEs Rewire Their Business Chemistry with Governance

Did you know that by 2025, 34% of Swiss SMEs already use AI tools, but only a third of them have clear rules for their use? The chemical industry exemplifies how Advanced Analytics and Generative AI can rewire growth – but without the right governance, potential quickly turns into an incalculable risk.

The AI Boom in Swiss SMEs: More Than Just Hype

The figures are impressive: In 2025, the proportion of Swiss SMEs using AI tools rose from 22% to 34%. At the same time, 37% of companies actively tested AI applications. This is no longer a futuristic vision, but a reality in many businesses, from small manufacturers to medium-sized service providers. A full 57% of surveyed SMEs reported improved efficiency through AI, a significant jump compared to the previous year (46%).

This development is a clear sign: AI is not a luxury for large corporations, but a crucial lever for competitiveness and growth in the Swiss SME sector. The 'Rewiring for Growth' initiative, which we observe in the chemical industry, for example, symbolises how companies are fundamentally redesigning their processes, products, and business models. It's not just about incremental improvements, but about a reconfiguration for a data-driven future.
